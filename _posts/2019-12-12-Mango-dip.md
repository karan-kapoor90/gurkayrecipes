# Mango Dip

## Ingredients

1. 2 cups fresh cut mango
2. 1/2 cup raw mango
3. 1/2 tea spoon ginger
4. 10 basil leaves
5. 1/2 garlic pod
6. 1 red chilly
7. 1/2 lemon
8. 2 tea spoons honey
9. 1/4 tea spoon salt


## Method

1. In a blender, put all ingredients and blend well.
2. Serve chilled.
